STYLE: ASDF Movie-style animation frame. Every frame is drawn by the same hand with the same tools — a medium-weight black marker on flat white paper. The look is clean, minimal, instantly readable, and expressive through simplicity.

FIGURES: Pure stick figures. Head = a simple circle (not oval). Body = one vertical line. Arms = two angled lines from shoulder point. Legs = two lines from hip point, ending in small oval feet. Eyes = two dots (larger apart = surprised, smaller = neutral, X = KO). Mouth = small curve up (happy), curve down (sad), small circle (shocked/speaking), flat line (neutral). No nose. No ears. No detailed hair unless needed for character identification.

CHARACTER CONSISTENCY: If a character reference sheet is provided, match that character's design EXACTLY in every frame they appear — same head size, same proportions, same identifying features. Never omit a character's identifying marks. Characters must look identical across all frames they appear in.

BACKGROUND: solid flat white (#FFFFFF). No texture, no paper grain, no shadows, no atmosphere. Environment is suggested with 1–3 minimal geometric props only: a single horizontal line = ground/table, a rectangle = door/window/wall, a triangle = mountain. Lots of empty white space. When a scene continues in the same location as the previous frame, reuse the exact same background props (same position, same style).

COLOR: flat fills, no gradients, no shading, no blending. Colors can be used freely — clothing, props, emotional color-washes on shapes, backgrounds panels. Up to 3–4 flat colors per frame besides black and white. The primary accent color is specified per frame.

COMPOSITION: wide 16:9, subject centered, generous white negative space, ONE clear action or emotion readable at a glance. If two characters interact, place them facing each other with space between.

SPEECH / TEXT: only when specifically requested in the scene description. Hand-lettered style, short, inside a simple oval speech bubble.

DO NOT: no realistic anatomy, no detailed faces, no gradients or shadows, no 3D, no photorealism, no anime/manga, no complex rendered backgrounds, no thick outlines (medium weight only), no watermarks, no copyright logos, no extra limbs, no broken anatomy.
